Image segmentation
for breast cancer

The detection of tumors in the breast depends on segmentation techniques. Our aim in this project is to showcase the performance of different segmentation algorithms to help detect tumors and classify them. These masses can come in different shapes and different shapes entail different diagnostics; the tumor can be benign or malign.
